About Me

I grew up in New York and moved to Texas in 2015. I sometimes miss the mountains of Upstate New York but have come to love the warm weather of Texas and the friendly people I have met here. When I'm not working with computers, I am playing guitar or hiking around new places with my girlfriend and our dog, Rosie.

I have been playing guitar for 12 years and studied music theory in school. I grew listening to metal music but I've learned to appreciate jazz and folk music more. I have been in and out of bands since I started playing and recently have been giving lessons to younger kids so they can start their own bands and share some of the amazing experiences I had when I was growing up.

Before getting into Web Development, I worked in the Mental Health and Psychology fields for 6 years. There was a focus on working in teams and communication with not only our coworkers but the people we served as well. I enjoy providing support to people who really need it and would like to continue giving back to our community in any way I can.
